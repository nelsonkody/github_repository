Camp North Star

Camp North Star is an mixed martial arts camp located in the Bitteroot Valley, Montana. Through movement, fresh air, and humility, kids from all levels of experinece leave with improved confidence, valuble self defense skills, and a growing appreciation for their mind and their body's capabilities. Our experinced instructors and world class facilities will provide a safe enviornment for your child to build a stronger body and patient mind. Over the course of two weeks, kids learn and sharpen their striking, jiu jitsu, and wrestling skills. Mixed with plenty of outdoor activities and team builidng exercises, your children will also learn valuble social skills. Parents can attest that Camp North Star helped their child be more confident and level headed when faced with the incoming challanges of growing up and almost all attendees beg their parents to come back the next summer!
